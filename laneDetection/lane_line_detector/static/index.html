CONCEPT

The idea is to see how the back end of self driving care can be made to function with use of computer vision. Given a video feed, the algorithm outputs a video that clearly demarcates the curvature of the road ahead. This information can be provided to the driver as caution. Over and above this we can develop a system that replaces the driver and acts on the information received through a program.
